About Pet Net

Our mission is to connect loving families with pets in need.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Pet Finder started as a small community project to help local shelters find homes for animals. We believe every pet deserves a safe and loving environment, and our platform is dedicated to making that connection as easy as possible.

We partner with shelters and rescue groups across the country to showcase animals who are looking for their forever homes. Thank you for considering adoption!

Our Team

We are a small team of animal lovers, designers, and developers passionate about animal welfare.
